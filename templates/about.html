About IPL Analytics Dashboard

A comprehensive, data-driven sports analytics platform delivering deep insights into historical teams, player careers, venue records, and head-to-head cricket rivalries.

Project Overview

The IPL Analytics Dashboard is a high-performance web application designed to bring you the deepest possible insights from the Indian Premier League (IPL).

By parsing raw match dataset histories, the platform transforms complex cricket records into interactive graphs, statistics, and metrics.

Whether you want to analyze batting strike rates, bowling averages, stadium trends, or inspect H2H matches, the application offers an intuitive interface tailored for cricket enthusiasts, analysts, and researchers alike.

Key Platform Features

Team Analytics

Analyze team performance, winning trends, venue-specific records, and season-by-season statistics.

Player Statistics

Detailed profile pages for batsmen and bowlers showing matches, strike rates, economy, and dismissals.

Head-to-Head

Side-by-side rivalry analysis, head-to-head records, win margin details, and historical comparisons.

Match Insights

Venue performance logs, average scores, toss impact, decision trends, and match results.

Our Purpose

This dashboard was developed to demonstrate the practical application of sports data analytics. By integrating robust data science workflows with modern front-end engineering, we aim to deliver an accessible repository of IPL history, allowing everyone from casual fans to analytical developers to make sense of the match statistics and performance trends.
